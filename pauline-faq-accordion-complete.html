› How much do I need for a down payment?
The required down payment depends on your loan type:
  • 1% Home program: Pauline's in-house program allows for as little as 1% down.
  • Conventional loans: As little as 3% down for qualified borrowers.
  • FHA loans: Typically 3.5% down.
  • VA and USDA loans: 0% down.
With down payment assistance programs, many buyers can purchase a home with little to no money down.

› What credit score do I need to buy a home?
While requirements vary by loan program, many homebuyers can qualify with a credit score as low as 580 for FHA loans. Conventional loans typically require a higher score (620+). Alternative loan programs are available for qualified individuals with credit scores as low as 500. Pauline can provide guidance on improving your credit to qualify for better rates.
